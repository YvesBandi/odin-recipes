Ingredients

  • 2 medium onions
  • 1 a piece of fresh ginger
  • 1 a piece of fresh turmeric
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 1 teaspoon grama masala
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 2 tablespoon cold pressed olive oil
  • 2 cups dry black lentils
  • pinch of salt and pepper
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • fresh herbs
  • a handful of ripened tomatoes
  • 1 cup carrot (1 medium size)
  • 1 lemon

Instructions

  1. Start with cleaning your lentils and leave them to drain all water
  2. In a pan bring the spice on low-heat as you chop the onions
  3. Add chopped onions in the spices, add oil and increase the heat to medium-high, stir and cover
  4. Grate turmeric, ginger, and garlic using a vegetable grater. Add them to the onions and stir
  5. Cook the onions in spices for 2-3 mins covered
  6. Bring in the lentils, mix well, add salt and the broth
  7. Increase the heat till the boiling take place, then reduce to medium-high
  8. Let the lentils simmer till the broth is absorbed
  9. Shred the carrot and dice the tomatoes and fresh herbs to make a salad
  10. Add pepper and squeeze the lemon juice on the lentils. Mix gently and let them cool
  11. Mix the cooked lentils with carrot-tomato salad, mix them gently
  12. Adjust the seasoning
  13. Serve this delicious black lentils salad with rice, bread, quinoa, or serve a large serving for a light meal
